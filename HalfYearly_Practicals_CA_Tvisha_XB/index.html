FAQs

  1. What are the operating hours of the animal park?
    The animal park is open daily from 9 AM to 5 PM, allowing plenty of time to explore and enjoy our attractions.
  2. Are outside food and beverages allowed?
    Outside food and beverages are not permitted in the park to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all visitors.
  3. Do you offer guided tours?
    Yes, we offer guided tours led by knowledgeable staff who provide insights about our animals and conservation efforts.
  4. Is there a parking facility available?
    Yes, we have ample parking available for visitors, with designated areas for cars, buses, and accessible parking.
  5. Can we bring pets to the animal park?
    Unfortunately, pets are not allowed in the park to protect our animals and ensure a safe environment for all guests.
